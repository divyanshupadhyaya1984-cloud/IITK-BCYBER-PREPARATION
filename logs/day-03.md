## 🚀 Obstacles & Breakthroughs
* *Obstacle:* Confusing the operational purpose of Encoding vs. Hashing vs. Encryption.
* *Breakthrough:* I mapped them out mathematically based on their mapping properties: **Encoding** is a bijective data transformation for transport efficiency (reversible without secrets); **Hashing** is a many-to-one deterministic mathematical function creating unique fixed-width fingerprints (non-reversible); **Encryption** is a mathematical transformation requiring a specific cryptographic key parameter to compute the inverse function. This structural framework resolved all confusion.
